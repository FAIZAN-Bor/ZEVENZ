Project Overview

ShopMax is a comprehensive e-commerce platform built for a growing retail business that needed to expand their online presence. The platform handles everything from product catalog management to order fulfillment and customer analytics.

The Challenge

The client needed a scalable e-commerce solution that could handle high traffic, integrate with existing inventory systems, and provide detailed analytics for business insights. The old system was outdated and couldn't handle the growing customer base.

Our Solution

We developed a modern, responsive e-commerce platform using React for the frontend and Node.js for the backend. The solution includes real-time inventory management, secure payment processing, and comprehensive analytics dashboard.

Results

The new platform resulted in a 150% increase in online sales, improved customer satisfaction scores, and reduced cart abandonment by 40%. The admin dashboard provides real-time insights that help drive business decisions.
